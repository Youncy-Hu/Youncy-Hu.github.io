About Me

 I am a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering at Wuhan University, China, supervised by Professor Zhenzhong Chen. I received my B.Eng. degrees at Wuhan University in 2017. I am fortunate to have an internship in Microsoft Research Asia during 2021-2022 working on video generation.
My current research interests include computer vision and image/video processing, especially focusing on generative models, visual reasoning, and video quality assessment.
